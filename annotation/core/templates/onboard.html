Real or Fake Text?

The goal of this research project is to understand what makes human-written text sound good and what makes computer-generated text sound bad. If you're familiar with the Turing Test, then you will be playing our subject in a similar experiment!


You will be presented a human-written prompt and a sequence of sentences that continue the prompt's text. At some point after the prompt, the text will begin to be written by a computer. Your goal is to select when you think the text is written by a human or a computer as soon as you are confident in your choice. Some signs of computer-generated text include poor grammar, non-sensical meaning, and frequent repetition.
