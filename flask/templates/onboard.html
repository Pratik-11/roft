Welcome and thank you!

The goal of this research project is to understand what makes human-written text sound good and what makes computer-generated text sound bad. To do this, you will read a passage of text, sentence-by-sentence and tell us when you think the passage is being written by a computer. Some signs of computer-generated text include poor grammar, non-sensical meaning, and frequent repetition.

If you're familiar with the Turing Test, then you will be playing our subject! Feel free to ask questions if you are having difficulty with completing the experiment, or if there were any trials in particular that you thought were notable.
